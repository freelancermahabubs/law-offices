Kamrul Hafiz Ahmed - Lawyer in Toronto, Ontario

Kamrul Hafiz Ahmed has been offering professional legal help since 2005 for Immigration Law, Real Estate Law, Criminal Law, Family Law, and Wills & Estates.

Contact our lawyer at the Kamrul Hafiz Ahmed Law Office in Toronto, ON for all your legal needs.

We are a team that is committed to providing you legal support. We approach every client with a focus on integrity, advocacy and understanding. You will receive quick responses to all your inquiries. Kamrul Hafiz was called to the Ontario Bar in 2005 and has a Master's degree in Laws and a Post-Graduate Diploma in Regulatory Law Administration. Kamrul has over 10 years of extensive experience in residential and commercial real estate transactions, commercial leasing and private lending. His clients include individuals, business entrepreneurs, developers, and financial institutions. Kamrul's law office also provides personal support throughout the entire immigration and application process, whether you are pursuing temporary or permanent residency, a work permit or student visa or any of the various paths to immigrate to Canada.
